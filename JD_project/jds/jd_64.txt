64. NetSuite Accountant – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Perform day-to-day accounting in NetSuite
- Manage GL, AP, AR, and revenue modules
- Ensure accurate financial reporting and dashboards
- Support month-end close and reconciliations
- Customize and maintain NetSuite workflows and reports
- Coordinate with NetSuite consultants and auditors

Skills:
- Strong NetSuite ERP

Experience: 2–6 years
Education: Degree in Accounting or Finance
