66. Accounting Manager – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Manage general ledger, AP, AR, and fixed assets
- Oversee month-end and year-end closing processes
- Ensure compliance with accounting standards and policies
- Review financial statements and reconciliations
- Lead and mentor accounting team members
- Coordinate audits and resolve audit observations

Skills:
- Strong accounting and people-management skills
- ERP and advanced Excel knowledge

Experience: 6–10 years
Education: Degree in Accounting or Finance; CA/CMA preferred
