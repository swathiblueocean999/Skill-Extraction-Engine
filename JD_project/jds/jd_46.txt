46. Audit Manager – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Plan and manage audit engagements
- Supervise audit teams and review work
- Evaluate risks and internal controls
- Present audit findings to senior management
- Ensure compliance with auditing standards
- Manage client and stakeholder relationships

Skills:
- Strong leadership and project management skills
- Extensive audit and compliance knowledge

Experience: 6–10 years
Education: CA/CIA/CPA qualification preferred
