78. Company Secretary (Finance-Focused) – Job - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Ensure compliance with corporate and financial laws
- Manage board meetings and statutory records
- Support corporate restructuring and governance
- Liaise with regulators and stakeholders
- Advise management on compliance matters

Skills:
- Qualified Company Secretary (CS)
- Knowledge of finance, compliance, and governance
- Strong documentation and advisory skills

Experience: 4–10 years
Education: None
