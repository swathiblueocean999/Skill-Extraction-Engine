57. MIS Analyst – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Design and maintain MIS dashboards and reports
- Analyze trends, variances, and performance metrics
- Automate reporting processes where possible
- Work with finance and operations teams for data alignment
- Provide actionable insights to management

Skills:
- Advanced Excel and BI tools knowledge

Experience: 2–5 years
Education: Degree in Finance, Accounting, or Data Analytics
