27. International Tax Accountant – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Handle international tax compliance and reporting
- Apply Double Taxation Avoidance Agreements (DTAA)
- Support cross-border transactions and structuring
- Coordinate with global tax teams and advisors
- Assist in transfer pricing documentation

Skills:
- Knowledge of international tax regulations

Experience: 4–8 years
Education: Accounting/Finance degree; CA preferred
