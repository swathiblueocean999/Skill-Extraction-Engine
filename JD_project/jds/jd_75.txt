75. Public Practice Accountant – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Maintain client books of accounts
- Prepare financial statements and tax returns
- Conduct audits and compliance reviews
- Provide accounting and tax advisory services
- Liaise with clients and regulatory authorities
- Stay updated on accounting and tax laws

Skills:
- Strong client-handling and compliance knowledge
- Multi-client and time-management skills

Experience: 3–8 years
Education: Accounting/Commerce degree; CA preferred
