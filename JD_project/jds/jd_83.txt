83. NGO / Trust Accountant – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Maintain books of accounts for trusts/NGOs
- Ensure compliance with donor and regulatory requirements
- Prepare utilization and financial reports
- Support audits and donor reviews
- Manage grant and fund accounting

Skills:
- Knowledge of nonprofit accounting and compliance
- High integrity and transparency

Experience: 2–6 years
Education: Accounting/Finance degree
