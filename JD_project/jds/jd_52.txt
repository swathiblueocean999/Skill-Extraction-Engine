52. Payroll Manager – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Manage end-to-end payroll operations
- Ensure compliance with labor laws and regulations
- Review payroll accuracy and approvals
- Lead and mentor payroll team members
- Handle payroll audits and escalations
- Implement payroll process improvements

Skills:
- Strong payroll and compliance expertise
- Leadership and stakeholder management skills
- Advanced Excel and payroll system knowledge

Experience: 6–10 years
Education: Degree in Accounting, Finance, or HR; professional certification preferred
