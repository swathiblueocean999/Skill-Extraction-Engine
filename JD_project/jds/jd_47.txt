47. Compliance Auditor – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Conduct compliance audits across functions
- Review adherence to laws, regulations, and policies
- Identify compliance gaps and risks
- Prepare compliance audit reports
- Track corrective and preventive actions

Skills:
- Strong regulatory and compliance knowledge
- Detail-oriented and documentation-focused

Experience: 3–6 years
Education: Degree in Accounting/Finance
