38. Accounts Receivable Accountant – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Maintain AR sub-ledger and reconciliations
- Post revenue and AR journal entries
- Analyze AR aging and bad debt provisions
- Support month-end and year-end close
- Assist in audits and financial reporting

Skills:
- Strong understanding of revenue and AR accounting
- ERP and Excel proficiency

Experience: 3–6 years
Education: Degree in Accounting or Finance
