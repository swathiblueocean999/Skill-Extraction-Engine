51. Payroll Accountant – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Process payroll accounting entries and accruals
- Reconcile payroll expenses with general ledger
- Ensure compliance with statutory payroll regulations
- Prepare payroll-related reports and schedules
- Support payroll audits and statutory inspections

Skills:
- Strong understanding of payroll accounting
- Knowledge of labor laws and statutory compliance
- ERP and Excel proficiency

Experience: 3–6 years
Education: Degree in Accounting or Finance
