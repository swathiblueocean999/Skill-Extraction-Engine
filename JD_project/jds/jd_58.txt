58. Financial Reporting Analyst – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Prepare monthly, quarterly, and annual financial reports
- Ensure compliance with accounting standards and regulations
- Reconcile financial data and resolve discrepancies
- Support statutory and internal audits
- Maintain reporting documentation and schedules

Skills:
- Strong financial reporting and compliance knowledge
- High attention to detail and accuracy

Experience: 3–6 years
Education: Accounting or Finance degree
