56. MIS Executive – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Prepare daily, weekly, and monthly MIS reports
- Collect, validate, and analyze data from departments
- Maintain dashboards and performance trackers
- Ensure data accuracy and report timeliness
- Support management with ad-hoc reports

Skills:
- Strong Excel and data handling skills
- Attention to detail and reporting accuracy

Experience: 1–3 years
Education: Graduate in Commerce, Finance, or Business
