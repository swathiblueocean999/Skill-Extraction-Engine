42. Internal Auditor – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Conduct internal audits as per approved audit plans
- Review internal controls, policies, and procedures
- Identify process gaps, risks, and control weaknesses
- Prepare audit reports with findings and recommendations
- Follow up on audit observations and corrective actions
- Coordinate with management and stakeholders

Skills:
- Strong understanding of internal controls and risk

Experience: 3–6 years
Education: Degree in Accounting/Finance; CA/CIA preferred
