35. Accounts Payable Executive – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Process vendor invoices and verify supporting documents
- Match invoices with purchase orders and GRNs
- Prepare payment runs and track due dates
- Resolve vendor queries and discrepancies
- Maintain AP records and aging reports
- Assist in month-end closing activities

Skills:
- Knowledge of AP processes and controls
- Proficiency in MS Excel and accounting software

Experience: 1–3 years
Education: Graduate in Accounting / Commerce / Finance
