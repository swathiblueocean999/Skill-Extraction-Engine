53. Compensation & Benefits Analyst – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Analyze compensation structures and salary benchmarks
- Support annual compensation reviews and increments
- Administer employee benefit programs (insurance, bonuses, incentives)
- Prepare compensation reports and dashboards
- Ensure compliance with compensation-related regulations
- Support HR leadership in reward strategy decisions

Skills:
- Advanced Excel and reporting tool knowledge
- Understanding of compensation frameworks and benefits

Experience: 3–7 years
Education: Degree in Finance, HR, or Business Administration
