43. External Auditor – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Plan and execute statutory and external audits
- Examine financial records and supporting documents
- Assess compliance with accounting standards and laws
- Prepare audit working papers and reports
- Discuss audit findings with clients and partners
- Support regulatory and statutory filings

Skills:
- Strong knowledge of auditing standards
- Client handling and documentation skills

Experience: 2–5 years
Education: Accounting/Finance degree; CA preferred
