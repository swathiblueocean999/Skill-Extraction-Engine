50. Payroll Executive – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Process monthly payroll for employees
- Collect and validate attendance, leave, and salary data
- Calculate salaries, deductions, incentives, and reimbursements
- Ensure statutory deductions (PF, ESI, PT, TDS)
- Maintain payroll records and reports
- Respond to employee payroll-related queries

Skills:
- Basic knowledge of payroll laws and compliance
- Proficiency in Excel and payroll software
- High accuracy and confidentiality

Experience: 1–3 years
Education: Graduate in Commerce, Accounting, Finance, or HR
