12. Accountant – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Maintain books of accounts and general ledger
- Record journal entries and reconcile accounts
- Prepare monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements
- Handle accounts payable and receivable
- Support tax filings and statutory compliance
- Assist in audits and financial reviews

Skills:
- Knowledge of accounting standards and taxation basics
- Proficiency in accounting software and MS Excel

Experience: 2–5 years
Education: Bachelor’s degree in Accounting/Finance/Commerce
