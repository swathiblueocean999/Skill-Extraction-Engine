74. Chief Financial Officer (CFO) – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Define and drive overall financial strategy
- Oversee accounting, finance, taxation, treasury, and compliance
- Lead capital planning, fundraising, and investor relations
- Manage enterprise risk and governance frameworks
- Support CEO and Board in strategic decisions

Skills:
- Exceptional leadership and strategic thinking
- Strong

Experience: 25+ years
Education: CA/CPA/MBA in Finance
