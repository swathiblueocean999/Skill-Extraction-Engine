3️. Accounting Associate – Job Description
Role Overview:
The Accounting Associate ensures accurate recording of transactions and supports accounting
operations across departments.
Key Responsibilities:
• Maintain accurate financial data and records
• Process invoices and expense claims
• Reconcile accounts and resolve discrepancies
• Assist with audits and compliance requirements
• Support financial documentation and reporting
Required Skills & Qualifications:
• Bachelor’s degree in Accounting or Finance
• Strong analytical and organizational skills
• Proficiency in Excel and accounting tools
Experience Level:
0–2 years
