4️. Trainee Accountant – Job Description
Role Overview:
The Trainee Accountant is an entry-level learning role focused on gaining hands-on exposure
to accounting processes and financial operations.
Key Responsibilities:
• Learn bookkeeping and accounting processes
• Assist in data entry and documentation
• Support senior accountants in daily operations
• Learn tax compliance and financial reporting
• Participate in training and mentorship programs
Required Skills & Qualifications:
• Commerce/Finance graduate or pursuing CA/CMA
• Willingness to learn accounting systems
• Basic computer and Excel knowledge
Experience Level:
Fresher / Internship / Training role
