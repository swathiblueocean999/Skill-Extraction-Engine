2. Junior Accountant – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Prepare and maintain financial records
- Assist in accounts payable and receivable processes
- Post journal entries and update ledgers
- Assist in tax preparation and statutory filings
- Help with monthly and quarterly financial reports

Skills:
- Basic understanding of GST/TDS (preferred)
- Knowledge of accounting software (Tally/Zoho/SAP – preferred)

Experience: 0–2 years
Education: Degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
