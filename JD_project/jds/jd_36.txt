36. Accounts Payable Accountant – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Manage end-to-end AP accounting
- Reconcile vendor statements and AP ledgers
- Post journal entries and accruals
- Ensure compliance with tax and accounting standards
- Support audits and internal controls

Skills:
- Strong knowledge of accounting principles
- ERP/accounting software

Experience: 3–6 years
Education: Degree in Accounting or Finance
