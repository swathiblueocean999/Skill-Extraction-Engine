11. Payroll Assistant – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Assist in payroll processing and calculations
- Maintain employee payroll records
- Support statutory deductions (PF, ESI, PT, TDS)
- Respond to payroll-related employee queries
- Assist with payroll audits and reports

Skills:
- Basic payroll and labor law knowledge
- High confidentiality and accuracy

Experience: 0–2 years
Education: Graduate in Accounting, Finance, or HR
