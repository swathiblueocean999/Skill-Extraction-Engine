5️. Accounts Executive – Job Description
Role Overview:
The Accounts Executive manages day-to-day accounting activities including transactions,
reconciliations, and coordination with vendors and clients.
Key Responsibilities:
• Handle accounts payable and receivable
• Maintain ledger accounts and records
• Prepare invoices and follow up on payments
• Support GST, TDS, and compliance filings
• Coordinate with auditors and internal teams
Required Skills & Qualifications:
• Bachelor’s degree in Accounting or Finance
• Knowledge of accounting software
• Strong communication and coordination skills
Experience Level:
1–3 years
