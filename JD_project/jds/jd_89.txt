89. GST Consultant - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- GST registration and return filing (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, etc.)
- Reconcile GST data with books and portals
- Advise on GST applicability, rates, and input tax credit
- Handle GST notices, audits, and assessments
- Support clients during GST department queries
- Ensure compliance with GST amendments and rules

Skills:
- Strong expertise in GST laws and procedures

Experience: None
Education: Practical
