1. Accounts Assistant – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Assist in maintaining books of accounts
- Record daily transactions, vouchers, and journal entries
- Support bank reconciliation and ledger maintenance
- Assist in preparing invoices and expense reports
- Maintain accounting documents and files
- Support month-end closing activities

Skills:
- Basic knowledge of accounting principles
- Proficiency in MS Excel
- Attention to detail and accuracy

Experience: 0–2 years
Education: Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or Commerce
