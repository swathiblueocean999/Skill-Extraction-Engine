68. Accounts Manager – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Supervise accounting operations and staff
- Ensure timely bookkeeping and reconciliations
- Manage statutory compliance and tax filings
- Review financial statements and MIS reports
- Coordinate with auditors and regulatory authorities

Skills:
- Strong compliance and accounting knowledge
- Team management

Experience: 6–10 years
Education: Accounting or Commerce degree; CA preferred
