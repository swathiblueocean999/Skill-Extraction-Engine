61. ERP Accountant – - Job Description

Responsibilities:
- Perform accounting transactions within ERP systems
- Maintain general ledger, AP, AR, and fixed assets in ERP
- Ensure accurate configuration and usage of accounting modules
- Support month-end and year-end close processes
- Assist in ERP data validation and reconciliations
- Support audits and financial reporting using ERP data

Skills:
- Strong understanding of accounting processes
- Hands-on

Experience: 2–5 years
Education: Degree in Accounting or Finance
