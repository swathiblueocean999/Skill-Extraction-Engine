Zecpath AI – Demo Dataset Notes
================================

Project:
AI Hiring & Evaluation System

Purpose:
--------
This dataset is created to simulate a complete AI-based hiring pipeline for accountant-related job roles.

Dataset Includes:
-----------------
1. Job Descriptions
2. Candidate Responses
3. ATS Scores
4. Screening Results
5. HR Interview Answers
6. Technical Interview Answers
7. Final Candidate Scores

Folder Structure:
-----------------

datasets/
│
├── candidate_responses/
│   ├── hr_answers.json
│   ├── screening_answers.json
│   └── technical_answers.json
│
├── job_descriptions/
│   ├── accountant_jd.json
│   ├── junior_accountant_jd.json
│   └── senior_accountant_jd.json
│
├── responses/
│   ├── strong_candidates.json
│   ├── average_candidates.json
│   └── weak_candidates.json
│
└── unified_candidate_scores.json

Candidate Categories:
---------------------
1. Strong Candidates
   - High ATS score
   - Strong accounting knowledge
   - Good communication
   - High technical accuracy

2. Average Candidates
   - Moderate accounting skills
   - Basic technical understanding
   - Average communication

3. Weak Candidates
   - Low ATS score
   - Missing technical skills
   - Poor accounting fundamentals

Supported Accounting Domains:
------------------------------
• General Accounting
• Accounts Payable
• Accounts Receivable
• Taxation
• GST
• Payroll
• Financial Reporting
• Audit
• MIS Reporting
• ERP Accounting

Simulation Goals:
-----------------
• Validate AI hiring pipeline
• Test ATS scoring consistency
• Evaluate screening AI
• Simulate HR interviews
• Simulate technical interviews
• Compare candidate quality
• Generate hiring recommendations

Dataset Rules:
---------------
• All candidate IDs are unique
• Scores are normalized to 100
• Strong candidates score above 80
• Average candidates score between 55–79
• Weak candidates score below 55

Scoring Components:
-------------------
• ATS Score
• Screening Score
• HR Score
• Technical Score
• Communication Score
• Final Recommendation Score

Environment:
-------------
Designed for:
• Demo Simulation
• AI Testing
• Pipeline Validation
• Recruitment Workflow Analysis

Status:
-------
Dataset Ready for End-to-End AI Simulation
