# Communication Evaluation Notes

## Fluency
Checks sentence continuity and response length.

## Grammar
Checks basic grammar quality.

## Vocabulary
Measures word variety and advanced usage.

## Clarity
Measures explanation quality and relevance.

## Structure
Checks logical sentence organization.

## Fillers
Detects filler words like:
- umm
- uh
- actually
- basically

## Bias Reduction
Uses normalized scoring to reduce unfair evaluation.
